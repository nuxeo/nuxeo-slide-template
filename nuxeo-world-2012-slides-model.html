Small things to know before starting

You need to follow a list of simple rules to keep consistency in all the Nuxeo Training slides.

  • Each slide is a section and needs to have the class="slide"
  • and each slide needs to have an id to have a unique URL
  • The id must be in lowercase letters, as you can see in the current URL before-starting
  • Always start your slides with an opening slide
  • and end it with an ending one
  • Try to always be precise and concise
  • Don't hesitate to add links from a slide to another, thanks to anchors

How to write a slide

Titles - The h3 title is mandatory in each slide

  • The h1 title is only used for the opening slide. It's the strong black title.
  • The h2 title is only used for the separation slides. It's the strong blue title.
  • The h6 title is only used to display author's names on a yellow background.

Nuxeo Enterprise Platform helps you build Content Applications to effectively manage your Content, but what does this mean?

The software that you and your users use to manage and collaborate on content. It can be any business application. Complete your slides with comments

Once your training is over, you may want to give your slides to your trainees, with additionnal info, tips & tricks. But you don't want to make them visible during the training, so the slides are not saturate with too much info.

Follow these steps to have 2 modes in your slides :

  1. Write your comments in a div class="further-info"
  2. This class will display your div by default
  3. Comment the nuxeo-theme-with-comments.css line in the head tag
  4. Your comments are now invisible :)

Check if your comments are on or off before starting your training!

Exercises

Exercise theme (1/2)

Add the class class="slide exercise" to the section to display the exercise theme.

  1. Be nice with your trainees,
  2. use an ol list type for the exercises,
  3. to help them find their way when you speak.
  4. If you say slide # point 2,
  5. it's easier to find it with the number next to it,
  6. rather than simple bullets.

If you have many slides for an exercise, please add the status numbers in the h3 title and add it in the id of your slide, to get unique anchors for your links.

Animation

To animate something, just add the class slide to your container. It can be a div, a li, ...
